My name is Nicolas Wong and I have a Bachelor of Science degree in graphic design from the Illinois State University

I am a graphic designer and an artist, born in Jakarta, Indonesia. I am currently attending Illinois State University to pursue my major in graphic design/UX design. I have fallen in love with drawing and art since I was little, and pursuing this into the digital world is another whole level of challenge that I cannot wait to conquer. My goal is to use my art skills in the real world and have the ability to create my own uniquely designed website. My target is to work at big companies, such as Apple, Google, Riot Games, etc.



Besides creating artpieces, I also love to create content and play video games. I create a lot of TikTok content & stream on Twitch from time to time to connect with other content creators, or even just to chat with viewers. Having the ability to create digital arts allows me to make my own streaming designs the way I want to.
