ABOUT

NICOLAS
WONG

Nicolas Wong, was born and raised in Jakarta, Indonesia, where he spent the first 12 years of his life. The move to the United States brought numerous challenges, as he had never spoken the English language before. Arriving as a seventh-grader, Nicolas embarked on a journey to learn English from scratch, facing the difficulties of comprehending and expressing himself in a new language. Amidst these struggles, he discovered solace and a means of communication through the world of art. In his art class, Nicolas found a sanctuary where he could express himself and connect with others despite the language barrier. It was during this formative period that his passion for arts and design are formed, laying the foundation for a remarkable journey ahead.



Their ultimate goal is to utilize their creativity and technical expertise in designing distinctive and visually captivating websites that effectively represent their clients' brands. They are continuously seeking opportunities to enhance their skills and stay at the forefront of the ever-evolving field of graphic design. As they progress in their professional journey, their aspiration is to collaborate with the most innovative companies in the industry. They are excited to discover the path their talents and passions will lead them to and eagerly anticipate applying their unique perspective and skills to every project they undertake.

Besides creating art pieces, I have a great passion for content creation, as it allows me to connect with people, get to know them better, and it brings a smile to my face.
