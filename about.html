My name is Nicolas Wong and I have a Bachelor of Science degree in graphic design from the Illinois State University

As a talented graphic designer and artist born in Jakarta, Indonesia, I am passionate about transforming my creative vision into stunning visual designs. I am currently pursuing my major in graphic design/UX design at Illinois State University, where I am gaining valuable skills and knowledge to take my artistic pursuits to the next level. From a young age, I have been enamored with drawing and art, and I have now honed my skills in the digital realm to meet the unique challenges of this exciting medium. My ultimate goal is to use my creativity and technical expertise to design unique, eye-catching websites that capture the essence of my clients' brands. I am constantly seeking new ways to improve my skills and stay ahead of the curve in the rapidly-evolving field of graphic design. As I continue on my professional journey, my dream is to work with some of the most innovative companies in the industry, such as Apple, Google, and Riot Games. I am excited to see where my talents and passions will take me, and I look forward to bringing my unique perspective and skills to every project I tackle.



Besides creating artpieces, I also love to create content and play video games. I create a lot of TikTok content & stream on Twitch from time to time to connect with other content creators, or even just to chat with viewers. Having the ability to create digital arts allows me to make my own streaming designs the way I want to.
