K/DA

5x5 in - Photoshop - 2021


This is a really fun project that I worked on for my favorite game, League of Legends. The music group K/DA is definitely, hands down, the best one out there. This piece focuses on how the characters will pop out of the shape, creating that 3D effect into it. I played around with lots of masking and layering.
