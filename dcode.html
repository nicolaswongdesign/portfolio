Dcode

Mobile - XD - 2022


The goal of this project is to create a translation app that can break the language barrier for people who are having a hard time understanding. This app prototype includes 3 main purposes, visual, hearing, and slangs. The app does not only focused on the purpose, but also their accessibility in mind. This includes text to speech feature and colors that are suitable for any visual disabilities.
