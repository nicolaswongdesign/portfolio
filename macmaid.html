Macaroni Maids

9x12 in - ILLUSTRATOR, INDESIGN, PHOTOSHOP - 2021


This project is about creating a fake real-time branding, and it is one of my favorite projects to work on so far. I not only created a brand, but we also created physical pocket folders for them. The folder includes information about the company, what they sell, where they are located, etc. The shapes of the folder and the stepsheets are customized to match with the brand.
