Katliente X Jinx

7.5x5 in - PHOTOSHOP - 2021


With a combination of my hobbies on League of Legends and streaming, I paired one of my favorite streamers, Katliente, who helped me a lot with streaming advice, with Jinx from Arcane, the best tv show out there (totally not biased). I played around a lot with this piece's coloring and opacity filters. I also had to use my tablet to create those graffiti fonts and brushes. The client was thrilled with the finished product.
