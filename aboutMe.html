ABOUT ME

In 2016, Nicolas pursued design as his career, making his mark in the field. By 2020, he was actively engaged in design work.

My Backstories

I was originally born in Indonesia, where I spent my first 12 years. Moving to the US was a significant challenge for me, especially since I had never really spoken English before. One thing I miss the most is the food they have there. Despite encountering many unfamiliar things, I learned to adapt and embrace them.

My Life Goals

My biggest goal is to live a happy life without worrying about financial issues or responsibilities (in a positive way, of course). To reach that point, I am working on self-improvement to demonstrate my worth to the world and advance in my career.

My Hobbies

I do things beyond designing, and it might be hard to believe, but it's true. One of my main hobbies is playing video games, for which I am truly grateful. Gaming helps me navigate my lowest points in life and offers an escape from reality. My other hobbies include cooking, traveling, and content creations.
