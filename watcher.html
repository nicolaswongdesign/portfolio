Watcher

5x5 in - Photoshop - 2021


I created this project when the Marvel TV show "What If" was trending. I fell in love with how the stories/movies we knew could have a whole different plot if some small factors are changed. In this piece, I played with many different low opacity and dark lighting to create a feeling of being observed by the watcher at all times.
