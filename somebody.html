Somebody

12x10 in - ILLUSTRATOR - 2020


This piece features a portrait of my friend playing the guitar. I always loved the polaroid theme because of how pleasing to see and clean they are, especially with the margins around the work. I used many pen tools in this piece and layered them in the correct order. It took a lot time to find the right main parts to make the piece as minimalist as possible, but it turned out as I envisioned.
