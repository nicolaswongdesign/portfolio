Project Details

These aren't just single projects; they represent my work throughout my year as a social media intern at the Illinois State University Office of Admissions. My role involved creating appealing and engaging social media posts for future incoming students. Each post had a unique style while still following a set guideline.

Project Goals

To understand and apply an existing brand guideline while implementing new social media strategies to boost audience engagement. I also aimed to understand what makes social media posts entertaining and how to encourage more interaction. Additionally, I wanted to learn about social media algorithms on platforms I was not familiar with, and create a consistency in posting times.

Choosing the correct media sizes for different social media platforms is crucial so your images display properly without getting cropped. When creating a post that includes multiple slides, make sure they flow seamlessly together to give a cohesive and unified feel.

Adding motion to a static image by creating a video capturing more of the audience's attention, which help to boost your engagement and expand your content's reach. When implementing text into a video, you must consider the time it takes to read each phrase, knowing when to transition to the next one. The text durations should align with the average viewer's reading speed for optimal impact.

Creating a sense of presence by making the virtual experience for the user to feel included. For example, using snow for the winter merch promotion. Then, include a call to action at the end, this can bring user to go directly to where the advertise takes easier. By both using inclusion effect and call to action, it increased both user's attention and engagement rate.

REFLECTION

What I learned

Before diving into new projects or posts, I've learned to check the data analytics of previous posts, using A/B testing to understand what works and what doesn't. Beyond following guidelines, I also consider visualizations and the audience's attention span. Crafting post descriptions is equally important for effective communication and calls to action.

What could be improved

Can use more of a consistent style and aesthetic that enhances the overall page, making it more unified and recognizable as the brand. Other than improving visual elements, my thoughts on the post descriptions could be more active and include better calls to action.
